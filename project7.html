Amazon Prime Video Dashboard (Tableau)

Interactive Tableau dashboard providing insights into Prime Video’s global content library, enabling smarter content acquisition and audience engagement strategies.

Problem Statement

Prime Video leadership lacked a consolidated view of content performance across genres, release years, and countries. Existing static reports were fragmented and failed to provide actionable insights for content planning, recommendation engines, and regional strategies.

Approach

  • Collected and cleaned data covering content type, release year, ratings, and genres across multiple countries.
  • Built Tableau dashboards with filters for country, genre, and release year, enabling flexible exploration.
  • Applied trend analysis and storytelling visuals to highlight opportunities for expansion and optimization.

Key Insights

  • Action & drama dominate globally, but niche genres like documentaries and stand-up comedy are rising in certain regions.
  • Release patterns during holidays and summer drive significantly higher engagement.
  • Emerging markets show underrepresentation of localized content, signaling growth potential.

Impact & Business Outcome

The dashboard empowered Prime Video teams to make data-driven content acquisition and marketing decisions. They can now:

  • Customize recommendations by country and genre to increase relevance.
  • Time releases for periods of peak engagement, improving subscription retention.
  • Save 15+ hours weekly by replacing manual static reporting with interactive dashboards.
