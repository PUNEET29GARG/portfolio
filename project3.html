E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Python-based EDA and a KPI dashboard comparing YTD vs PYTD, category and region level performance.

Problem Statement

Leadership teams lacked a unified, interactive view of sales performance. They struggled to compare current-year metrics against prior year, which limited their ability to prioritize product investments and optimize marketing spend.
E-commerce stakeholders also faced challenges in tracking product performance, customer behavior, and evolving sales trends. Existing static reports were time-consuming to produce and did not provide the agility needed to act on demand fluctuations or market opportunities.

Approach

  • Consolidated sales data, cleaned records and created consistent product/category mappings.
  • Built KPIs for sales, profit, orders and market share. Visualized YTD vs PYTD and category trends.

Key Findings

  • Identified select categories with significant growth (greater than 300% uplift in highlighted categories during the analysis period).
  • Spotlighted regions with fastest customer acquisition and categories with high repeat purchase rates.

Business Outcome

The insights allowed product and marketing teams to reallocate budget to high-growth categories, adjust inventory planning, and design retention campaigns for high-value segments — decisions directly tied to revenue growth rather than just reporting.
