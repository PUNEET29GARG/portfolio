Credit Card Transaction Report — Power BI

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard to help financial stakeholders identify revenue drivers, optimize credit offerings, and understand customer spending patterns.

Problem Statement

The bank lacked a consolidated view of cardholder spending, revenue distribution, and customer demographics. This gap prevented targeted marketing, accurate forecasting, and effective revenue optimization.

Approach

  • Built KPIs for revenue, interest earned, and transaction volume.
  • Segmented performance by card type (Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum) and demographics (education, profession, income).
  • Enabled drill-downs for quarterly and annual transaction trends.
  • Used DAX for profitability ratios and customer retention metrics.

Key Insights

  • Blue card holders generated the largest share of revenue despite being the lowest tier.
  • Graduates contributed the most to total revenue among education groups.
  • Businessmen dominated high-value transactions, while salaried employees showed higher transaction counts.
  • Seasonal spikes were observed at fiscal year-ends, highlighting promotion opportunities.

Business Outcome

  • Targeted offers designed for profitable customer groups.
  • Improved quarterly revenue forecasting accuracy.
  • Optimized credit limit allocation and marketing spend.
  • Identified high-churn segments early for retention strategies.
