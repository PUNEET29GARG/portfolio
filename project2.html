Call Center's Trends — Power BI

Interactive Power BI dashboard for volume, abandonment and staffing insights.

Problem Statement

Contact center leaders lacked consolidated visibility into hourly volumes, daily peaks and abandonment. This caused under/over-staffing and poor SLAs.

Approach

  • Aggregated call logs and agent data; created hourly, weekday and campaign-level views.
  • Built KPIs for volume, average wait time, and abandonment; added filters to drill by queue and date range.

Quantitative Findings (from dashboard)

  • Observed ~20% call abandonment during peak windows and clear Monday morning volume spikes.
  • Identified specific hours when staffing levels did not match incoming call volume.

Outcome & Actions

The dashboard enabled targeted staffing optimization — shifting resources to peak hours and reducing abandonment. Stakeholders can now plan weekly schedules and run scenario analyses to evaluate staffing vs SLAs.
