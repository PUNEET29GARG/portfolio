IPL Data Analysis

Python analysis of match & player-level data to identify performance drivers and help franchises make data-driven decisions.

Problem Statement

Teams and analysts needed quantitative evidence about which player metrics or match conditions (toss, venue, powerplay performance) influence match outcomes most reliably.

Approach

  • Engineered features such as strike rate, economy rate, win ratios and venue-level averages.
  • Used grouping and aggregation to surface players/teams with consistently high impact metrics.

Insights

  • Players with strong powerplay strike rates and bowlers with low economy in middle overs have outsized influence on wins (insights surfaced in visualization).
  • Venue-level patterns (e.g., high-scoring grounds) help in tactical selection and match planning.

Outcome

Franchises can use the analysis to prioritize player recruitment by metric (not just reputation), design batting/bowling orders for specific venues, and inform broadcast narratives around match-up advantages.
